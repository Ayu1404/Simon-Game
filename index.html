Press A Key or Click Start to Play

How to Play

1. Press any key or click the Start button to start the game.

2. Watch the sequence of colors.

3. Repeat the sequence by clicking the colors in the correct order.

4. Each round adds one more color to the sequence.

5. Try to remember the sequence as long as you can!
